Decree 1421 of 2017
Boosting by the Minister of Education, Yaneth Giha Tovar. This means that the population with SEN (Special Educational Needs) can have access, permanence and educational quality "to be able to study from preschool to higher education, as well as education for work and human development".
Inclusive Education as a District Educational Policy
The result of the aforementioned process leads to guaranteeing the educational transit for Inclusive education from preschool to higher education, articulated with labor productivity through FUNDAC's strategic alliances with higher education and work education entities.

Services

Our Services

Performing curricular adaptations, campaigns and events associated with disability and formal education through training and accompaniment with all actors in the educational community.

Neuropsychology
Implementation of PIARs, classroom planning and PMI and cathedra for peace and citizen skills for human development and social transformation.
Psychosocial
Current regulations in relation to the training and school guidance component. Futhermore, Routes and protocols of integral attention - alliance family school law 1098 and Review, adjustment, training and implementation of the coexistence manuals Law 1620.
Common Educational
PEI oriented to diversity and curriculum by competences, curricular adaptation with the means and environments conducive to the advancement of being, managing to meet their particular needs.
Individual Educational
Knowledge in each student of the dimensions of human development, multiple intelligences, emotional intelligence, respecting the processes, strategies and learning styles.
Special Educational
The evaluation, the pre-diagnosis, the curricular adaptations and the personalized project. With a trained and qualified interdisciplinary team that manages to efficiently, effectively and effectively address the needs of each student.
